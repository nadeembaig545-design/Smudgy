— Our Story

Paper still
matters.

Smudgy was born out of a love for the imperfect — the ink that bleeds a little, the page that wrinkles with thought, the planner with every messy crossed-out plan. We make stationery that celebrates the human in you.

Based in Hyderabad, crafted with care, shipped all over India. Every product is a small invitation to slow down and create something real.
